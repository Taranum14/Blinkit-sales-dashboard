# 📊 Blinkit Sales Performance Dashboard (Excel)

### *If you were running Blinkit, what would you want to know on a Monday morning?*

That's the question that started this project. I took a Blinkit-style outlet and sales dataset and built an interactive Excel dashboard to answer it — turning raw rows of data into a story about where the business is winning, where it's surprising, and where it might be slowing down.

## 📖 The Story Behind the Numbers

**The Tier 3 surprise.** I expected big metro (Tier 1) outlets to dominate sales. Instead, **Tier 3 locations led with $472.1K** in total sales — ahead of both Tier 2 ($393.2K) and Tier 1 ($336.4K). Smaller markets are punching above their weight.

**Medium beats "High."** Outlet size doesn't scale the way you'd assume — **Medium-sized outlets generated 42% of total sales**, more than "High" category outlets. Bigger isn't always better.

**Groceries over snacks.** On a platform built for instant delivery, I expected snack and impulse categories to lead. Instead, **Fruits & Vegetables topped the item-type rankings**, ahead of Snack Foods and Household items — suggesting people trust Blinkit for everyday essentials, not just cravings.

**The 2018 peak.** Outlet sales climbed steadily from 2011, peaked at **$204.5K in 2018**, then cooled in the years after — a single chart that hints at a growth curve hitting market saturation.

Put together, the data tells a story that adds up to **$1.2M in total sales, 8,523 items, and a 4.0 average customer rating** — explorable by anyone through the dashboard's interactive filters.

## 🧰 Tools & Techniques Used

- Microsoft Excel
- PivotTables & PivotCharts
- Slicers (Outlet Size, Outlet Location, Item Type)
- Donut & Bar Charts
- KPI summary cards using formulas (SUM, AVERAGE, COUNT)
- Dashboard layout & design

## 🚀 How to Explore It Yourself

1. Download `Blinkit_Dashboard.xlsx`
2. Open in Excel (enable editing if prompted)
3. Use the **filter panel** on the left — Outlet Size, Outlet Location, Item Type — to dig into the data and find your own stories
4. Watch the KPIs and charts update live based on your selections

## 📈 What's Inside

- **Filter Panel:** Slice by Outlet Size, Outlet Location, and Item Type
- **KPI Cards:** Total Sales, Average Sales, No. of Items, Average Rating
- **Outlet Establishment Trend:** Year-wise sales trend, 2011–2022
- **Fat Content Breakdown:** Low Fat vs Regular, by outlet tier
- **Item Type Analysis:** Sales by category — Fruits & Vegetables, Snacks, Household, and more
- **Outlet Size & Location Breakdown:** Sales split across Tier 1/2/3 and High/Medium/Small outlets
- **Outlet Type Comparison:** Total Sales, Avg Sales, and No. of Items by outlet format
